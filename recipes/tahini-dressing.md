### Ingredients

- ⅓ cup good quality tahini (stir the jar first)
- 3 lemons, juiced
- 1 garlic clove, grated on a microplane
- 3 tbsp water, plus more to thin
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp honey or maple syrup

### Steps

#### Whisk together
Whisk the tahini with the lemon juice — it will thicken dramatically at first. Add the garlic, salt, cumin, and honey. Keep whisking while slowly adding water until the dressing is smooth, creamy, and pourable. It should coat a spoon but still flow easily.

#### Adjust and rest
Taste and adjust — more lemon for brightness, more water to thin, a pinch more salt if it tastes flat. A little cayenne or smoked paprika is a lovely addition. Let it sit 5 minutes before serving; the garlic mellows as it rests.

### Notes

Tahini quality matters enormously — Soom, Seed + Mill, or Al Wadi are excellent choices. The dressing will seize up when you first add lemon; keep whisking and adding water a tablespoon at a time until it loosens into a creamy, pourable consistency. Stores in the fridge for a week. Thin with water as needed before serving.
